About Marrakech

Marrakech, an ancient imperial city in western Morocco, is a major economic center adorned with mosques, palaces, and gardens. The medina is a densely populated medieval fortified city dating back to the Berber Empire, featuring labyrinthine alleys where vibrant markets sell fabrics, pottery, and traditional jewelry. The city's symbol, the 12th-century minaret of the Koutoubia Mosque, stands tall and is visible from afar.
